Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the unplugged retreat?

You have to take a 5 hour train from the Amtrak station in Bern. It is a long time so please bring food and water. Jungle Book will be playing througout the ride so you will be entertained

What size teams can you accommodate?

We can accommodate no more than a 100. After that, it will be more like a party than a retreat.

What kind of team is right for the unplugged retreat?

The kind of team right for the retreat is one that loves nature, and does not mind cockroaches in the cabin, or having to occasionally port a potty. They should expect a simplistic life, e.g. no fancy dinners or prom dresses.
